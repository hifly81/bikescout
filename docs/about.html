What is BikeScout?

BikeScout isn't a corporate product. It's for riders who got tired of the "black box" nature of modern cycling apps.

We started this project in April 2026 to bring transparency to trail data. The goal was simple: raw data for raw riders.

Philosophy

We believe that planning a mission should be as technical as the ride itself. BikeScout is designed for who care about soil density, gradient analysis, and real-time weather vectors.

By open-sourcing the MCP Server, We are inviting other developers and explorers to contribute to a standard where trail intelligence is accessible, customizable, and above all, truthful.
