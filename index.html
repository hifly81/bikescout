Why BikeScout?

Filtered Elevation

Stop trusting "fake" vertical gains. Our progressive filtering eliminates satellite noise for realistic elevation metrics.

MTB-Scale Parsing

We decode technical OSM tags (S0-S5). Know if a trail requires trial skills or if it's a smooth gravel cruise.

Multi-Discipline Logic

Custom algorithms for Road, Gravel, and Enduro. Effort scoring that adapts to your bike's rolling resistance.

Trail Scouting

Deep analysis of surfaces and waytypes using OSM and OpenRouteService.

Weather Guard

Cycling-specific forecasts with technical advice on wind and rain risk.

Native MCP

Zero-config integration for Claude Desktop, Cursor, and the MCP ecosystem.
