Who is behind the code?

BikeScout isn't a corporate product. It's for riders who got tired of the "black box" nature of modern cycling apps.

With a background in software engineering and a passion for pushing bikes where they "don't belong," I started this project in April 2026 to bring transparency to trail data. The goal was simple: raw data for raw riders.

Our Philosophy

I believe that planning a mission should be as technical as the ride itself. BikeScout is designed for the 1% who care about soil density, gradient analysis, and real-time weather vectors.

By open-sourcing the MCP Server, I'm inviting other developers and explorers to contribute to a standard where trail intelligence is accessible, customizable, and above all, truthful.

"Innovation is found in the dirt, not the boardroom."
